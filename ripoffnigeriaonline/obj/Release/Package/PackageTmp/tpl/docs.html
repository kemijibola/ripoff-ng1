What is rip-off Nigeria?

rip-off Nigeria is a service to Nigerian consumers. The platform enables YOU the consumer to be armed with the views and comments of other consumers like you. Posting a report against companies, governments, businesses, individuals that have treated you unfairly is your right to freedom of speech which should be exercised without fear of being reprimanded.

How do I file a report on rip-off Nigeria?

You must be a registered member to post a report or add rebuttal to a report rip-off Nigeria.
If you are already a registered member, Select the "File Report" button

  • Enter your username
  • Enter your password

You will be redirected to File Report page which consists of 5 simple steps.

  • Give all the Company or Individual's vital information, names they go by, complete address, phone numbers, e-mail and web address.
  • Give your report a Topic and categorize it.
  • Additional details is required to add more information about the business transaction.
  • Write your report in detail.
  • You may upload supporting images, submit form by clicking the complete button.

What makes a good report?

The report should be relating an honest, factual, and impartial story. Make your statement as objective as possible. You should be aware that reporting a false statement about a company or an individual can get you SUED. Use any details such as names, times, places, tape recorded evidence (where legal), pictures, and witnesses statements, that will reveal the facts and support your Report. These items could be emailed to us at reports@rip-offnigeria.com to be made a part of your actual report.

How secure is my personal information?

Your personal information including filed reports are strictly confidential. We will not sell your e-mail address or any other information that you have given us. We simply use it to identify the complaint you have posted. At the most, we may e-mail you notifications concerning reviews of interest, events and consumer tips.

Are reports on rip-off Nigeria ever censored?

We do not provide or create any content in the reports or the titles. So go ahead, speak your mind! We will not edit, censor, or manipulate your report on rip-off Nigeria in any way. We provide this service for you. We believe it is imperative that information listed on this site is unedited and straight from the source.

Can I update my report on rip-off Nigeria?

If your complaint is resolved or situations get even worse, you can always UPDATE your report on rip-off Nigeria with the updated information. Reports can only be updated and not removed.
