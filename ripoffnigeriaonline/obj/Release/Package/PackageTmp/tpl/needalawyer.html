How It Works

LOGIN

01
Login to file your report against an individual or a company. If you are not a member yet, register to become one.

POST YOUR REPORT

02
Click on File Report and complete the form which consists of 5 simple steps.

Provide as much detail as possible about your report.

TAKE LEGAL STEPS

03
Click on My Filed Reports to view all reports filed by you.

On the Legal Action column, click on
Get a Lawyer for the report you seek redress and complete the form.

PAYMENT

04
You will be contacted via Email/Telephone after 24 hours of successful transaction. It's Simple!
